I am Phd candidate at the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at University of Tennessee, Knoxville, advised by Dr. Jian Liu. My research focuses on AI security, federated learning, and generative models, with a particular interest in privacy-preserving and trustworthy machine learning systems and smart healthcare. My research work has been published at top-tier mobile-computing/AI/security/bio-medical venues (e.g., S&P/Oakland, AsiaCCS, IJCAI, ACM UbiComp, and EMBC)
